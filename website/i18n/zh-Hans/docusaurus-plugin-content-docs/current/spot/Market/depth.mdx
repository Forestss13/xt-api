**类型:** GET **描述:** `/v4/public/depth`

### 参数

| 名称 | 类型 | 必填 | 默认值值值值 | 描述 | 范围 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| symbol | 字符串 | 是 |  | trading pair eg:btc_usdt |  |
| limit | 数字 | 否 | 100 | minimum 数字 of queries is 100 | 1~500 |

#### **Limit Flow Rules**

10/s/ip
