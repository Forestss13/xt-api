Subscribe：

\{"channel":"ex_group_market","group":\{Group\},"event":"addChannel"\}

Unsubscribe：

\{"channel":"ex_group_market","group":\{Group\},"event":"removeChannel"\}

## 请求参数

| 名称 | 类型 | 必填 | 默认值 | 说明 | 范围 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `group` | string | 否 | all | Group type | all or Trading area（eg：USDT）or trading pair(eg: BTC, USDT) |
